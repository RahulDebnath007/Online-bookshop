Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I log in to my account?

A: Click on the "Login" button in the navigation bar and enter your email and password to access your account.

Q: What if I forget my password?

A: Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Q: How do I sign up for a new account?

A: Go to the "Sign Up" page, fill in the required details, and create your account.

Q: Who can I contact for further assistance?

A: Check the contact options below to reach us directly.
